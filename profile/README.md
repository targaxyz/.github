Targa keeps online communities clean. It begins as a Telegram anti-spam bot and grows into a full security layer for the places people gather.

## The product

One bot, three tiers, one unified dashboard:

- **Targa** — *free.* Classical, non-LLM anti-spam: fast, deterministic, the foundation every community gets.
- **Targa M2** — *paid.* Smart-routed LLM detection layered on top — the LLM only sees the messages the rules can't decide, so it catches more without costing more.
- **Targa X1** — *premium.* A dedicated AI agent assigned to your channel: it sees context, takes action, drafts warnings, reports back. A guard who never sleeps.

## Why Targa

- **Built for modern spam.** Closes the gaps that defeat rule-based bots today: homoglyph handles, edit-after-post attacks, image-text scams, admin impersonation.
- **Explainable end-to-end.** Every action — classical, AI, or agentic — carries a structured reason. No silent bans.
- **Layered by design.** Rules, AI, and identity work as one coordinated stack, not a pile of single-trick bots.

## Where it's going

Wallet-based identity gating across TON, Polkadot, SUI, and Base — and, beyond moderation, a security company for digital communities: detection, threat intelligence, a live public Spam Index, and an in-house research arm.

## Status

Targa is in **private beta**. Access is invite-only while we harden the foundation.
