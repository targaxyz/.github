  **AI security for digital communities.**

---

Targa keeps online communities clean. It begins as a Telegram anti-spam bot and grows into a full security layer for the places people gather.

## The product

- **Targa** — *free.* Classical, non-LLM anti-spam: fast, deterministic, the foundation every community gets.
- **Targa M2** — *paid.* Smart-routed LLM detection layered on top, for the spam that slips past the rules.
- **Targa X1** — *premium.* A dedicated AI agent that watches your channel like a guard who never sleeps.

One unified dashboard manages all of it.

## Where it's going

Identity gating across TON, Polkadot, SUI, and Base — and, beyond moderation, a security company for digital communities: detection, intelligence, and a dedicated research arm.
